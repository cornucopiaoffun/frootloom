📖 How to Play

🎯 The Concept

Froot of the Loom is the party game about things your brain is absolutely sure about that turn out to be dead wrong. From Mandela Effects (shared false memories) to myths everyone believes, misquoted movies, debunked science, and "Wait, REALLY?!" moments — this game will make you question everything you think you know.

🎲 Gameplay

  • Each question has two choices — pick what's actually real
  • Questions span Easy, Medium, Hard, and 🍎 Wild Card
  • 463 questions — randomized every game, play dozens of times without repeats
  • After answering, you get the real story — prepare for "NO WAY" moments

💰 Scoring

  • Easy: 100 pts
  • Medium: 200 pts
  • Hard: 300 pts
  • 🍎 Cornucopia Wild Card: 500 pts

🔥 Streak Bonus

  • 3 streak: 1.5× multiplier
  • 5 streak: 2× multiplier
  • 10 streak: 3× multiplier

One wrong answer resets your streak. Brutal.

⚡ Double Down

Once per game, you can DOUBLE DOWN on a question before answering — doubling points earned (or earning nothing if wrong). Use it wisely.

🏆 Classic Mode Rounds

  • Round 1 "The Warmup": 5 easy Qs
  • Round 2 "Reality Check": 7 medium Qs
  • Round 3 "The Gauntlet": 5 hard Qs
  • Round 4 "🍎 Cornucopia Blitz": 3 wild cards
  • Round 5 "The Final Stand": 5 mixed, 1.5× points

🏅 Rankings

  • 👑 Memory Master: 90%+ — Darren-level
  • 🧠 Reality Resident: 75-89%
  • 🤔 Glitch Detector: 60-74%
  • 😵‍💫 Timeline Traveler: 40-59%
  • 😎 Full Channing: Below 40%

📸 How This Game Was Born

The Scene

Trivia night. Vibes: immaculate. Drinks: flowing. The boys: locked in. Then the question dropped.

"Does the Fruit of the Loom logo contain a cornucopia?"

The Debate

Channing — with the conviction of a man who has never been wrong — declared "YES. 100% cornucopia."

Darren — cool, calm, dangerously correct — quietly said "No. There isn't."

The Fallout

Google confirmed: NEVER a cornucopia in the Fruit of the Loom logo. Ever. Not once in the company's entire history.

Channing went through all five stages of grief in four seconds. Meanwhile, Darren was spotted making the most legendary victory faces known to mankind.

"NO WAY. I SWEAR ON EVERYTHING."
— Channing, moments before reality destroyed him

The Mandela Effect

Turns out, this is one of the most famous Mandela Effects in the world — millions of people "remember" a cornucopia that never existed. Channing isn't alone — he's just the most vocal about it.

This game — Froot of the Loom — was born from that night. Named after the very brand that started the argument, dedicated to every false memory, debunked myth, and misquoted movie line your brain has been lying to you about. It's a cornucopia of fun. Every question, every wrong answer, it all comes back to that one trivia night when reality won and Channing lost.
